Frequently Asked Questions

AniMalSync currently supports updates to both Anime and Manga entries. The following changes are supported:

  • Status
  • Score
  • Progress
  • Repeating
  • Times Repeated

Unfortunately, there are some features that are not/can not be implemented. For example, removing an entry from your AniList profile is not yet supported, as the API endpoint being used does not support that check. There is another endpoint that could be used, but it is currently not supported.

AniMalSync begins syncing all changes made after the auto-sync feature is enabled for your account. Therefore, if you have a fresh AniList account, you will want to import your MAL entries to your AniList account first before enabling auto-sync.

Alright, I've done that. Now what?
Log into your account and go to your profile page. Assuming you've verified your email and authorized AniMalSync to access your MAL account, enable auto-sync and you are good to go!

Roughly every month or so, the MyAnimeList OAuth tokens used by AniMalSync will expire, and a new authorization email will be sent to you so make sure to keep an eye out for that. Auto-sync will be disabled until the app has been authorized again.

Unfortunately, AniMalSync is closed and invite-only. The webapp is powered by AWS, and to avoid excessive charges on my account, I've decided to limit the amount of accounts that can be made. If you wish to sign up, send an email to animalsync.app@gmail.com and your request will be considered.

AniMalSync stores your email, AniList username, and MAL OAuth credentials to be able to function correctly. Sync logs are also stored on your account so that you can view the sync history and see if any sync attempts failed and require manual syncing. These sync logs are stored for only a week before they are completely wiped from the database. Other than that, no other information is stored.
